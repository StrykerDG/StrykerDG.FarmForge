Road Map

  • October 2020: MVP

    • Keep track of planted crops
    • Log observations, actions, and inputs for each crop you plant
    • Tie crops to a specific location

  • November 2020: Inventory Management

    • Keep track of current inventory
    • Add to inventory when harvesting a crop
    • Group inventory by units

  • January 2021: Order Managmenet

    • Keep track of customers and orders
    • Specify prices for items

  • March 2021: IoT Device Management

    • Ability to connect and view IoT devices
    • 2-3 Pre-built Devices
